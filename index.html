THE CINEPHILES

the movie making club of IIT Indore

We, at The Cinephiles, make short films and work on various other video related projects. All of us are ultimate movie freaks with passion towards various fields of movie making. Here, we enjoy the art of film making along with mastering the pillars of film making which includes direction, editing, script writing, sound mixing among others. Being a club of IIT Indore, we also aim to take the institute to new heights by showing our best ability in competitions like IITvsIIM and Inter-IIT any many others.
